About

The „PartyGame—Associations“ Web App is developed by Valeriia Kozenasheva and Han Yang, two Master Students at the University of Cologne, supported by the Institute of Digital Humanities.
The idea behind the project is to create a web game for the parties and let people get acquainted with each other in a short time. The game aims to bring out more interactions between players and different teams, and hopefully they can learn some words or enhance the communication ways while playing. The App is generated with the framework Angular CLI and written with TypeScript.
