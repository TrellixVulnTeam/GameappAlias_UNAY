About

The „PartyGame—Association“ Web App is developed by Valeria Kozenasheva and Han Yang, two Master Students at the University of Cologne, supported by the Institute of Digital Humanities. The idea behind the project is to create an easy web game as Ice-Breaker at Parties and let people get acquainted with each other in a short time. The game aims to bring out more interactions between players and different teams, And hopefully they can learn some words or enhance the communication ways whilst playing. Currently, the game is only available in web version, and the App is generated with Angular CLI, mainly written with TypeScript.
